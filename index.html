Round 2, Motherfucker. Fight!

You are the best character in the 2D beat-em-up adventure game of your life. You have the most powerful roundhouse kick, the best hidden moves and the most sought-after cheat codes.

But somewhere along the way, you lost your fire. You saw people claim ground you thought was yours by right, saw lesser mortals scale mountains and draw water from wells you thought were dry.

Round 1 was a tie. It’s time for round 2.

Round 2 is about two crucial things:

  • Repay your fortune

    Sometimes you’ll trip over a pot of gold. When that happens, recognise your fortune and pay it back by doing work that justifies the good luck you’ve received.

    Got a dream contract? Work that little bit harder to keep it. Found a bit of spare cash? Use it on something that’ll improve your life and/or work, or the lives of those around you.

    Recognise in others when they’ve been lucky and have paid it back. Respect them, and turn any petty envy you have into fuel.

  • Earn your place

    No matter how much you want something, no-one is just going to give it to you. You might get a lucky start, but now that you’re repaying that luck with effort, it’s time to work to your next big goal.

    Do this with determination and singular focus. Make the most of your productive hours, and be aware of those micro-breaks.

    Start your day right, with a clear head and a bit of exercise, and you’ll be ready to take the rough with the smooth.
